Know Us Better

Learn more about who we are and what drives us.

What inspired the creation of SmartFit?

SmartFit was born out of Lara Oliveira’s personal fitness journey. After becoming a mother of three, Lara found it increasingly difficult to maintain her fitness routine with traditional workouts. During a visit to her home country of Brazil, she discovered EMS training, which provided quick yet effective results in a short amount of time. Inspired by its efficiency and adaptability, Lara decided to bring this innovative fitness technology to Georgia, creating SmartFit as a solution for those who need to balance fitness with a busy lifestyle.

What makes SmartFit different from other fitness studios?

What truly sets us apart is our exclusive focus on Electrical Muscle Stimulation (EMS) training. Unlike other fitness studios that require long hours of repetitive workouts, we provide a full-body workout in just 20 minutes with EMS technology. This approach not only saves time but also activates deep muscle groups that traditional exercises often miss. We also pride ourselves on creating a welcoming, supportive community where everyone—regardless of fitness level—feels empowered and motivated to achieve their goals.

Who are the key people behind SmartFit?

Our team is led by our founder, Lara Oliveira, whose vision and passion for bringing EMS technology to the U.S. have been the driving force behind SmartFit. She’s joined by her daughter, Taylor Oliveira, a certified personal trainer with a background in collegiate soccer. Taylor’s deep understanding of EMS and her genuine dedication to helping clients reach their potential make her an invaluable part of our team. Together, we’re committed to guiding our clients through a transformative fitness journey.

How does SmartFit contribute to the community?

We’re more than just a fitness studio—we’re a community hub. We actively engage in local events and wellness initiatives in Alpharetta and Buckhead, aiming to promote a healthier lifestyle for everyone in our area. We also offer free trial sessions to introduce more people to EMS training, giving them a chance to experience its benefits firsthand. Our mission is to build a supportive environment where our members uplift one another and celebrate each other’s progress.

What values guide SmartFit’s mission?

Our mission is guided by three core values: innovation, inclusivity, and community. Innovation drives us to continually use the latest in EMS technology to create workouts that are both efficient and effective. Inclusivity means we’re dedicated to making fitness accessible to everyone, regardless of their experience level or physical limitations. Community is at the heart of everything we do—we’re here to support, motivate, and inspire each other to reach our fitness goals together.

How can I become a part of the SmartFit community?

We invite you to start by joining us for a free trial session so you can experience EMS training for yourself. From there, you can choose the membership option that best fits your needs. Don’t forget to download our app, where you can book sessions, track your progress, and stay connected with fellow members. Being part of SmartFit is more than just working out—it’s about joining a group of like-minded individuals who support and celebrate each other’s successes.
