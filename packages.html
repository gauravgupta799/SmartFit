Common Questions

Answers to help you understand our EMS packages.

What is included in the SmartFit membership packages?

Our packages offer two 20-minute EMS sessions per week. You'll get personalized training with certified professionals, along with access to our mobile app for easy scheduling, reminders, and updates on promotions.

Can I change or upgrade my package at any time?

Yes, we understand that fitness needs change over time. You can easily upgrade or modify your membership plan to a higher level, allowing more sessions per month with just a quick request to our support team.

What happens if I miss a session?

We know life can get busy. If you miss a session, you can reschedule it within the same week to ensure you don’t lose out on your training. Simply use our app or contact our team to find a convenient slot.

Are there any hidden fees or charges?

Absolutely not. Our pricing is fully transparent. The cost you see is all-inclusive of your training sessions, personalized support, and app features. There are no hidden fees to worry about.

Do you offer a trial before committing to a package?

Yes. We encourage all new clients to take advantage of our free trial session. It’s the perfect way to experience EMS training and see how it fits into your fitness journey before making a commitment.
